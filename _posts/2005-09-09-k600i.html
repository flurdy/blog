Camera is a beauty, not 2MP of the 750 and 800s, but 1.3 which is nearly as good. Crisp and clear. My old Treo 600 camera is ancient compared to it.

Just come back from holiday in Ibiza and used the camera on my phone more than my 5MP pentax proper camera.

Thank god I only know a few other people with 3G phones otherwise I'd waste too much money on video calls!

Only downside is that it does not have a full qwerty keyboard ala the Treo. But it cant have everything.
